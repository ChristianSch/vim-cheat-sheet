    Vim cheat sheet

    Go to

    Start of line

    0

    End of word

    E or e

    Cut & Copy

    Start “visual mode”

    • v and select chars
    • V for whole line

    Copy

    • y for selected chars
    • yy or Y for whole line
    • 2yy for two lines (nyy for n lines)

    Cut

    • d

    Paste

    • P before pointer
    • p after pointer
